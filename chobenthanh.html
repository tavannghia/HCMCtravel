Top3: Ben Thanh Market District 1 - The busiest market in Saigon

Address: Between the streets of Phan Boi Chau, Le Thanh Ton, Phan Chu Trinh, Quach Thi Trang Square, Ben Thanh Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City Opening hours: 7:00 – 19:00 Ticket price: Free

One of the tourist attractions in Saigon that many tourists check-in during their trip is Ben Thanh Market. This market is located in the city center, all four sides are adjacent to major roads, so it is very convenient for moving. For shopaholics, this is a destination not to be missed.
